Nearly Perfect Parent was created by James Perly, who has spent over 20 years intensely focused on personal growth and healing his own trauma. This firsthand experience developed James' knowledge of techniques like Internal Family Systems, brainspotting, somatic experiencing, psychedelics, and more. Now Nearly Perfect Parent leverages that learning to emotionally support you on your own journey, remembering your story and gently guiding you to find your own path.
